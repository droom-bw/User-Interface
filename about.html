Droom means dream in Dutch.

This app helps you find your dream job by swiping right.

Collaborating successfully as a team we have accomplished: - Onboarding processing for a job-seeker - Ability to easily create/setup a user profile (including name, occupation field, past experiences, interests, etc.) - Onboarding processing for companies - Ability to easily create / setup a company profile and current job listing - Ability to easily edit / update profiles and job listing - Ability to delete items - Ability for both user types to swipe right or left (or tap a check or x button) when they see a profile page or job posting/company page. - A page to view your matches. STRETCH - Ability to message a ‘match’. Ability to upload profile picture. - Allow login through LinkedIn to fill in profile.
